About Pogs

As two undergrads at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) pursuing different courses of study, Alfred (Pedong) and Regine (Gingin) never foresaw that their relationship would provide the perfect opportunity to tap into their entrepreneurial spirits and creative backgrounds.

In trying to find the perfect platform to collaborate and visualize their busy and often overwhelming schedules they found that no application existed that could meet the demands necessary. Still without a solution and disgruntled with overly complicated interfaces they decided to create the ideal application. Drawing inspiration from a present given to Regine by Alfred, the two set off to work. Thus Pogs was born.
